    HS000(Start) --> HS001[Ask the guest for their name]
    HS001 --> HS002[Ask the guest which hotel they are staying at]
    HS002 --> HS003[Ask the guest for their room number]
    HS003 --> HS004[Ask the guest what service they are requesting]
    HS004 --> HS005[Ask the guest when they would like the service]
    HS005 --> HS006[Process the service request by calling hotel_service_request API]
    HS006 -- Service request is successful --> HS007[Inform the guest that their service request was successful.]
    HS006 -- Service request fails --> HS008[Inform the guest that their service request failed.]
    HS007 & HS008 --> HS009[Ask the guest if there is anything else they need]
    HS009 -- Guest needs another service --> HS001
    HS009 -- Guest needs no further assistance --> HS010[End the conversation and thank the guest for using the service]
