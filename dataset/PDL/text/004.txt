1. Query the invoicing method for an order based on the order information provided by the user.
2. If the invoicing method is platform-issued:
   a. Query the invoicing progress.
   b. If no invoicing information is found, notify the user that no invoice information was found.
   c. If invoicing information is found, query the user's membership status.
   d. Execute different invoicing methods based on the user's membership card type and invoice type:
      i. If it is a Platinum Card:
         - Electronic General Invoice: Notify the user of the electronic general invoice content supported, and inform them of the sending method and time.
         - Paper General Invoice: Notify the user of the paper general invoice content supported, and inform them of the sending method and time.
         - Paper Special Invoice: Inform the user of the detailed information required.
      ii. If it is a non-Platinum Card:
         - Electronic General Invoice: Notify the user of the electronic general invoice content supported, and inform them of the sending method and time.
         - Paper General Invoice: Notify the user of the paper general invoice content supported, and inform them of the sending method and time, and inform them of the postage information.
         - Paper Special Invoice: Notify the user of the paper special invoice content supported, and inform them of the sending method and time, and inform them of the postage information.
3. If the invoicing method is hotel-issued, notify the user that the invoice has been successfully issued by the hotel.
